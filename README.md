# Small Business Automation Framework

Reusable architecture and workflows for small business automation in service-based industries.

## Goal
To provide a repeatable framework that helps small businesses automate lead capture, customer communication, intake, fulfillment coordination, and retention.

## Target sectors
- Food service
- Catering
- Wellness
- Local service businesses

## Framework pillars
1. Lead acquisition
2. CRM and qualification
3. Messaging automation
4. Operational workflow orchestration
5. Customer retention
6. Reporting and optimization

## Why this matters
Small businesses are a major part of the U.S. economy, yet many still operate with fragmented systems. This framework is designed to lower operational friction and support scalable growth through technology adoption.
